Marshall Garrison is a New York / Atlanta-based actor and stunt performer with years of character, voice, and stage combat training, coupled with real world weapons and firearms experience.  Growing up in a military family, he was fortunate to live and travel many parts of the world and immerse himself in various cultures.  Combining his upbringing with an extensive array of career fields such as rodeo, jousting, medicine, and the US Army; he uses this experience to craft his characters and bring realism and real world knowledge to his performances.  Whether scripted plays, immersive and improv shows, or tabletop gaming, Marshall loves incorporating the emotional depth necessary for characters to be authentic and memorable.
